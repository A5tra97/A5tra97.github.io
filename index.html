Biography

Autumn Astra Palm was born in Portland, Victoria, Australia, on the 9th of December 2002. From my youngest years I was always described as a puzzle solver and a thinker, often found to be asking questions people couldn't answer and trying to make things I probably shouldn't. I always laugh thinking back on when I was told that my best friends family would hide the matches when I visited since I had a tendancy to seek them out.
