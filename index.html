A Short Biography

Autumn Astra Palm was born in Portland Victoria on the 9th of December 2002. From early childhood I was interested in solving puzzles and building things. Most of my time as a kid was spent building Lego and reading, though later in my teenage years it evolved into software and robotics.

My childhood was rough, I didn't live with my parents since I had been placed in care with my nan because I was found covered in bruises at a few weeks old. I grew up blaming myself for this, for my dad not seeing me anymore, for the bullying I endured everyday and the fact I never seemed to fit in. Life inside the Jehovah's witnesses was no better, I was the kind of kid who questioned everything, and that kind of person doesn't blend well in cults. It was around 2017 when my dad died and I started to learn new things about myself. This lead to a very rough few years of leaving the cult I was raised in, questioning my reality, my identity. Eventually I ran away from my nan to live with my aunt and cousins, the abuse at home was getting too difficult to handle, especially with the new realisations about myself.

It led to a rough year, I cam out as trans, started transitioning, struggled with facing alot of my childhood trauma, including learning that my dad had stopped visiting me as a kid because of my nan, and not me, like I was told. I made my first realy friends and started actually finding myself and taking steps towards being able to have the life and career that I want.
